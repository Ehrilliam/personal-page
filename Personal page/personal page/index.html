About Me

Welcome I'm Ehren, dedicated UI and web developer. My journey into the world of web development began with a background in graphic design and 3D modeling, adding a creative twist to my technical expertise.

With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I'm all about creating user-friendly and visually appealing websites. I believe that the web should be both functional and beautiful.

My portfolio showcases web layouts and interactive games, giving you a taste of my passion for crafting engaging online experiences. I'm excited about the ever-evolving world of web development and the possibilities it offers.

Join me on this journey of learning, creating, and making the web a more vibrant and user-friendly place. I'm here to bring a touch of creativity and a commitment to excellence to every project.
